**Elhokar Kholin** was the king of #[Alethkar](locations/alethkar) on Roshar after his father. Son of late king  #[Gavilar Kholin](characters/gavilar) and #[Navani Kholin](characters/navani), he was also younger brother to #[Jasnah Kholin](characters/jasnah). He was married to Aesudan Kholin, and they had a children named Gavinor. He was murdered by #[Moash](characters/moash) during the assault to the Royal Palace, before he finished to pronounce the First Ideal and become a Knight Radiant.
